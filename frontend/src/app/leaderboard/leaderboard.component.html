About Co10 Escape

Escape is a co-op mission for ArmA 3 for up to 10 players. Your squad got tasked to scout out the enemy military presence. Unfortunately, you got caught and are now held in an improvised prison awaiting enemy force's officers to arrive for questioning.
However, allied insurgents managed to to hide a backpack with small weapons inside the prison. Your task now is to overwhelm the guards and escape the prison. Try to find a map of the island, make contact with your own forces, reach the designated evacuation zone and escape the island.

The mission is open source and supports various different islands and settings.
